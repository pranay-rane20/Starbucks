Caffe Latte

$ 20

Caffè latte is a popular Italian coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It's typically served in a tall glass or large cup and can be customized with various flavors like vanilla or caramel.

Strawberry mocha

$ 25

Strawberry mocha is a delightful coffee beverage that combines the rich flavors of chocolate and coffee with the sweet and fruity taste of strawberries. It's often made with espresso, steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and strawberry puree or syrup, topped with whipped cream and sometimes fresh strawberry slices.

Doppio espresso

$ 30

A Doppio espresso, also known simply as a doppio, is a double shot of espresso, resulting in a stronger, more intense coffee. It's made by extracting approximately 14 grams of coffee grounds with about 60 milliliters of water. This popular Italian coffee is the foundation for many espresso-based drinks.

Matcha latte

$ 20

A Matcha latte is a popular drink made from Japanese green tea powder (matcha) whisked with hot water and steamed milk, often sweetened. It boasts a vibrant green color and a rich, slightly bitter taste balanced by the creaminess of the milk. Matcha lattes are valued for their health benefits, including antioxidants and a gentle caffeine boost.
